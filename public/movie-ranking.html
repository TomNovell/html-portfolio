The Best Movies According to Tom

My Top 3 Movies of all-time.

Fight Club

It is filmed in a very unique way and has an incredible story line with plot twists and psycological awe.

Life Aquatic

This is my favorite Wes Anderson movie that features an epic ocean adventure, practical special effects and a fantastic cast.

Raging Bull

Martin Scorcese directs this boxing film staring Robert Deniro. It tracks the main characters life thru his rise and fall.
